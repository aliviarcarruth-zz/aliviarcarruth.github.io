HI, I'M ALIVIA

I am passionate about creating stylish and user friendly websites. I have been interning at IPAJAX as a Front-End Web Developer for the past six months. I also create websites for local businesses. Both have been huge learning experiences. Every day that I learn something new in programming is so exciting.

I am self-taught and began this journey a year and a half ago. To expand my skills, I have completed Colt Steele's Full Stack Web Developement Bootcamp and FreeCodeCamp's Responsive Web Design certificate.

I homeschooled and completed my B.A. 90% online so I am highly motivated and completely comfortable with remote work. My goal is to continue expanding my knowledge in order to build the best web applications with the latest technology.

Besides coding, I love spending time with my husband and young son. Any time my baby is napping, I am learning programming! It has been a long but exciting journey and I can't wait to see where it leads!
